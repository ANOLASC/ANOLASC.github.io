Learning efficiency

It is a infinity topic about how to learn efficiently, many people annoying that they are struggle to learn something new.

I have seen some idea about leaning efficiency from a youtube channel, that is to learn something new, we can obey some rules and use some learning paradigm which can be made to reduce some abundant learning phrase.

It says we must do some research about thing we gonna study, have recognition at least level. For example, suppose I gonna learn probability model. I need to do some research about what is probability model. I use Introduction to Probability Theory as a reference.
“Any realistic model of a real-work phenomenon must take into account the possibility of randomness. That is, more often than not, the quantities we are interested in will not be predictable in advance but, rather, will exhibit an inherent variation that should be taken into account by the model. This is usually accomplished by allowing the model to be probabilistic in nature. Such a model is, naturally enough, referred to as a probability model.”
